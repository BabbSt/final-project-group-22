When We Meet

ACM-W meets the 2nd, 4th, 6th, 8th, and 10th week of every term. Club meetings run from 5:30 pm - 6:30 pm PDT.

Where We Meet

ACM-W meets in Kelley Engineering Building in room 1005 at Oregon State University.
